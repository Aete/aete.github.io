Community Design project

Concepts

"A town that supports the needs of communities of various sizes, from individuals to blocks, through sharing."

Defining the requirement about public spaces and programs according to community size

Community Design

Community Road: Community roads pass through all community facilities, green space throughout the block.

Alley: Alleys are located between residential clusters(L). Alleys lead to existing community facilities at outside as well as new community facilities at inside the block.

Units

Dwelling units of community was designed consideration of the population changing of Korea. Detached Units are design for couples and families, and Share Units are design for students, entrepreneurs, and young employee of the start-up companies

Detached Unit : Unit for couple or family
Talent Share Unit : People who lives in this type unit are in charge of managing public facility which is located in ground floor.
Space Share Unit : Sharing several basic functions in house such as kitchen, living room, parking lot
Hobby Share Unit : Sharing not only basic functions but also some special programs for hobbies such as multimedia room, roof terrace, study room)
