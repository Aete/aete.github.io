I am an urban data scientist who loves analyzing the urban ecosystem with data. Also, I am interested in urban changes/innovations with cutting-edge technologies such as autonomous vehicles. I majored in Urban Informatics at NYU Center for Urban Science and Progress. I got a master's degree at NYU with a project about analyzing interagency work zone traffic data of NYC. Before that, I studied Architecture and Urban Design at Sungkyunkwan University. As a graduate researcher in SKKU Smart Green City Lab for more than 2 years, I had participated in several research projects about urban design and smart city policy & strategy. During that period, I got a master's degree at SKKU in 2019 with a thesis about the changes in neighborhood planning for the adoption of autonomous vehicles.
