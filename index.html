Seunggyun Han is a researcher who is interested in visualizing urban environments & phenomenas with data. Now, he is a graduate student in NYU Center for Urban Science + Progress. Before he came to NYU, He had studied architecture and urban design for several years in Sungkyunkwan University. In SKKU, he had participated in several research projects about urban design and smart city policy & strategy. He got a master's degree at SKKU in 2019 with thesis about the changes in the neighborhood planning for the adoption of the autonomous vehicle.
