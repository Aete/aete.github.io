Dwelling by Walling

 

Seunggyun Han, Taewon Choi, Jisoo Kim

Bronze Prize at Gyeonggi Architecture Culture Festival, 2016

Concept

Utilize space boundaries as a platform for Community

Planning Flexible walls which people can move and change by requirements of individuals and community.

Planning corridor between dwelling units as a specific program for living, working and playing

The corridor in the apartment has been used as a space for mobility without any programs. In this project, we suggested making entire of building as an urban ecosystem of 'Live, Work, Play' through utilizing the abandoned corridors

Strategy

Design the size of wall module by consideration of dimensions in daily activities and furniture.
